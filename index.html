About Our Art

Welcome to mehndi_work_, where tradition meets artistry and every design tells a story. I'm Jasmeen, a passionate henna artist based in Brampton, and after years of applying mehndi simply out of love for the craft, I’ve finally turned that passion into a business.

What began as a creative outlet has blossomed into mehndi_work_, a space where I bring intricate, custom henna designs to life, whether it's for weddings, festivals, parties, or just because you want to feel beautiful. With years of hands-on experience and a deep appreciation for the cultural richness of mehndi, I blend traditional styles with modern touches to create designs that are not just beautiful but meaningful.

This is more than just mehndi. It's a celebration of art, emotion, and identity. I’m excited to share this journey with you and be part of your special moments.

Let’s create something stunning together.
